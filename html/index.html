Convert Chrome DevTools Screenshots to animated GIF (video)

  1. Open Chrome DevTools
  2. Open the "Performance" Tab
  3. Activate the "Screenshots" checkbox
  4. Click "Record"
  5. Do stuff on the you want to see in the result video
  6. Stop recording
  7. Right click anywhere on the recorded timeline-> "Save Profile..."
  8. Upload a saved file via "Choose File..." button or just drag-n-drop it into the browser window
